	Customer Order
	
	In this tab, you may order in new products on behalf of a particular customer.
	
	To do this, select a product from the drop-down menu, specify a quantity to order (under "pc(s)"), and click the "Add" button.
	This will print to the screen a text copy of what you have just ordered.
	The "Total: " figure at the bottom of the screen shows the total cost (EUR) of the ordered items.
	Note that each product has a different number of copies available. This is specified beneath "Available: ".
	It is not possible to order more if this number is zero.
