	Supply Order History
	
	In this tab, you may view all orders placed in the Supply Order tab.
	These are sorted by date (in the format YYYY-MM-DD hh-mm-ss).
	A unique "Order ID" is assigned to each order. The supplier ID and staff ID are also given.
	To find out more about each order, simply click on the order. This will open a pop-up window.
	Under "Items in this order: ", the products, their respective quantities, and subtotal are listed.
	Underneath, the supplier's details are given again, along with a total order value.
	To return to the main application, click "OK".
