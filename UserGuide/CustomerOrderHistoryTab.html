	Customer Order History
	
	In this tab, you may view all orders placed in the Customer Order tab.
	
	These are sorted by date (in the format YYYY-MM-DD hh-mm-ss).
	
	A unique "Order ID" is assigned to each order. The customer ID and staff ID are also given.
	To find out more about each order, you may simply click on the order. This will open a pop-up window.
	In this pop-up window, you will see a list of the products constituting this order. A subtotal column gives the total cost of the required quantity of each product.
	The total value of the order is given at the bottom of the pop-up screen, beneath the customer's personal details.	
	
	To return to the main application, click "OK".
